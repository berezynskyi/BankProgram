SWIFT Transfers

SWIFT payments are a type of international transfer sent via the SWIFT international payment network. The SWIFT international payment network is one of the largest financial messaging systems in the world. TransferWise can send or receive certain currencies via SWIFT payment. It means we can bring our mid-market rate goodness to more places.
